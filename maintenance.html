Maintenance

The Oracle has an automatic cleaning procedure, making it as easy as 1, 2, 3.

  1. CLEAN ME! will be displayed on the LCD to indicate when a cleaning cycle is required (after approx. 200 shots). The cleaning cycle cleans the shower screen and back-flushes the group head.
  2. Insert the supplied grey silicone cleaning disc, followed by 1 cleaning tablet into the filter basket.
  3. Lock the portafilter into the group head.
  4. Ensure the water tank is filled with cold water and the drip tray is empty.
  5. Press the MENU button and rotate SELECT dial until 'CLEAN CYCLE' and 'PUSH' are displayed on the LCD.
  6. Press the SELECT dial to start the cleaning cycle.
  7. The LCD will display a countdown timer showing remaining time for the cleaning cycle in seconds.
  8. When the cleaning cycle has finished, remove the portafilter and ensure the tablet has completely dissolved. If the tablet has not dissolved, repeat these steps, without inserting a new tablet.
  9. Rinse the filter basket and portafilter thoroughly before use.
  10. Extract an espresso to "season" the machine and purge the system of any remaining cleaner.
