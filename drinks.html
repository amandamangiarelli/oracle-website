Drinks

A list of drinks to try with the Oracle espresso machine.

CAPPUCCINO

A single shot of espresso with textured milk and garnished with drinking chocolate.


ESPRESSO

Intense and aromatic, it is also known as a short black and is served in a small cup or glass.


LATTE

A latté consists of a single espresso with textured milk and approx. 10mm of foam. Very similar to a cappuccino.


MACCHIATO

Traditionally served short, the macchiato is also poured as a long black with a dash of milk or a dollop of textured milk.


AMERICANO

A shot of espresso (single or double) with hot water. Also known as Long Black.


RISTRETTO

A ristretto is an extremely short espresso of approximately 15ml, distinguished by its intense flavour and aftertaste.
